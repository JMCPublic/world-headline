The industrial hollowing-out

East vs West Germany

German memory culture

🔭 Sweden's Blind Spots

The gang shooting paradox
Sweden — one of the world's most equal societies — has Europe's highest rate of gang shootings per capita. The contradiction is vast and the explanation is genuinely complex. International coverage is usually shallow.
SD as kingmaker
The Sweden Democrats are not in government but effectively set its agenda. This arrangement — far-right outside, far-right agenda inside — is politically unique in Europe and almost unreported abroad.
NATO's psychological revolution
200 years of Swedish neutrality ended in 2024. The psychological shift for Swedes — particularly older generations who grew up with neutrality as a national identity — is enormous and rarely explored internationally.
Sweden's housing crisis
Sweden has one of Europe's most dysfunctional housing markets — queue times for rent-controlled Stockholm apartments can exceed 20 years. This shapes everything from inequality to gang recruitment. Barely covered internationally.
